♦ Lees McRae Cycling Team Update ♦

The Bobcats open their collegiate road racing season on March 27 with the Clemson race weekend. Lees McRae plans to host the SECCC conference road race championships on April 24th. They will have a 25 mile clockwise loop in Green Valley that starts and finishes at the elementary school in Green Valley using 19E, Squirrel Creek, Pyatte, 221 and Three Mile. It will be a road race and team time trial. This will be a collegiate only event with the hope to include open USA Cycling categories in the future if everything goes well this year and if volunteer and community support will support it.

Please contact Head Coach Tim Hall if you are interested in helping out.

♦ Updates from NICA ♦

The Watauga NICA Mountain Bike program began practices this week. Student Athletes are divided into "pods" based upon age and skill level that allow us to deliver coaching and a mountain biking community experience in a COVID-responsible way. We are thrilled with the growth of our program this year. We have 12 High School and 23 Middle School student athletes for a total of 35 participants! We also have an amazing group of 24 volunteer coaches this year! So, the NICA program will have 59 members of our local cycling community out on bikes every week through May. Say "hello" to our "pods" of young mountain bikers when you see them on area trails this Spring!
